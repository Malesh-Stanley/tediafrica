Empowering Women & Youth Through Social Innovation

Tedi Africa is a non-profit social enterprise dedicated to building just, prosperous, and sustainable communities in South Sudan and Kenya through technology, entrepreneurship, and social innovation.

Learn About Us Our Programs

Our Mission

To tackle poverty through social innovation, technology, and entrepreneurship for one million South Sudanese people by 2030.

Our Vision

To see Africa in which women & youth build just, prosperous, and sustainable communities through social innovation.

Our Values

Integrity, Innovativeness, Continuous Learning, Commitment to Service, Community Centeredness, and Quality drive everything we do.

Building Just, Prosperous Communities

Tedi Africa is a non-profit, non-governmental, multi-sectoral development social enterprise operating in South Sudan and Kenya, intervening in women & youth entrepreneurial space to advance sustainable solutions.

Entrepreneurship Development

Mentorship programs for refugee youth and women, providing enterprise support to small and emerging entities.

Refugee Livelihoods

Women's Economic Empowerment programs including capacity-building and vocational training for urban and camp-based refugee women.

Refugee Journalism

Leveraging the power of storytelling for social change through Kakuma Daily, a refugee-led citizen journalism initiative.

Youth Mentorship

Technical and educational support programs targeting both urban and camp-based refugee youth for personal growth.

Our Core Values

The fundamental principles that guide our mission and drive our commitment to empowering refugee women and youth

We are convinced that to deliver this goal, we need to uphold the highest degree of integrity. Being honest requires individual investment to be open, accountable, and transparent.

INTEGRITY

Foundation of Trust

TEDI cannot exist without the spirit of an innovative problem-solving mindset. We encourage innovative thinking towards education and the environment.

INNOVATIVENESS

Creative Problem-Solving

TEDI believes that education is a life-long goal. We promote and encourage ongoing learning opportunities.

CONTINUOUS LEARNING

Lifelong Growth

Selfless service is what drives our innovation. We encourage the spirit of volunteerism among our team.

COMMITMENT TO SERVICE

Selfless Dedication

Our activities cannot operate in a vacuum. We have a culture of shared prosperity, thus, involving our beneficiaries in every change we undertake. Community leads the process of social transformation. TEDI only guides the process.

COMMUNITY CENTEREDNESS

Shared Prosperity

Tedi aspires to create high-quality products and services. Creative disruption does not have to end up with low-quality programs.

QUALITY

Excellence in Everything
