Brief History

We came to be in June 2020 as a social venture committed to serving the youth and women using the triple nexus of Technology, Education and Entrepreneurship (TEE).

We were motivated by the need to act amidst a global pandemic that transformed how international development is designed and offered.

Like most, Covid-19 awoke the sleeping lion in us - We wanted to participate in the development field but wanted to do it differently.

The word 'Tedi' an acronym for 'Transform, Educate, Develop, and Initiate' represents a culture of innovation and creativity in the development paradigm.

Thus, the question 'Tedi?' a Nuer phrase for 'How is it?' evolved. In all that we do, we approach through a question before a solution.

In August 2021, we evolved to Tedi 'Africa' with the understanding that our vision is more than South Sudan.
