Transforming Lives Through Social Innovation

Since 2021, Tedi Africa has been at the forefront of refugee empowerment, partnering with global organizations and creating sustainable impact in South Sudan and Kenya through innovative programs and community-driven solutions.

Community Impact

Program Highlights

Our key initiatives are designed to create lasting impact for women and youth in Africa.

Entrepreneurship Development

Mentorship and enterprise support for refugee youth and women, helping them build sustainable businesses.

Refugee Livelihoods

Economic empowerment programs, capacity-building, and vocational training for urban and camp-based refugee women.

Refugee Journalism

Leveraging storytelling for social change through refugee-led citizen journalism initiatives.

Youth Mentorship

Technical and educational support for youth, fostering personal growth and leadership.
